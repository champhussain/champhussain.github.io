AZNIRULHISHAM MOHD HUSSAIN SUKOR is the CEO &
Senior Consultant of CMSB Human Capital Sdn Bhd and
focuses in management and soft skill programmes. He has
an MBA, a Bachelor of Commerce & Administration from
New Zealand and a Graduate Diploma in Management
from International Professional Managers’ Association
(IPMA), United Kingdom. Hisham first entered the working
arena as a P.A. to the Funds Manager with the Ministry of
Health and as an Operations Supervisor at TNT Skypak
International, both in New Zealand. Upon returning to
Malaysia, he started to give more focus on his career first
love: facilitating, mediating, consulting and most of all,
speaking to the general masses from all walks of life.
Hisham or fondly known as Champ by many of his family and close friends, started
his professional speaking career as a Master of Ceremony (MC) at many events
and occasions. He was the resident MC for Integrated Logistics Berhad (a private
listed company) where he worked as a Marketing Executive from 1991-1993. Earlier
during his career, he had numerous invitations to speak on various soft skill topics
and other personal-enhancement subjects to various organizations both private,
public as well as to NGOs. He was also a regular guest consultant on Radio 3 Shah
Alam, speaking on the subject of ‘excellence’ in 1993.
Amongst his notable programs included STAR CAMP (a full English speaking Team
Building program) for Malaysian Timber Industry Board (MTIB) apart from various
deliveries and facilitations to Government departments, GLCs, semi-government
bodies and agencies, universities and corporate companies.
Amongst the highlights of his career is when he was invited to speak on ‘Effective
Management & Transformational Leadership’ at Institut Latihan Kehakiman dan
Perundangan (ILKAP), Bangi of which the audience consisted of Judges from
various Session and High Courts from all over Malaysia as well as Deputy Public
Prosecutors from the Attorney General office.
He has also spoken to about 400 Customer Service Managers at CIMB Bank 3rd
Annual Symposium for Customer Service Managers on ‘Mindset & Behaviour
Empowerment’ at The Premiere Hotel, Klang. He has also conducted a series of
‘Supervisory Skills’ program for Tenaga Nasional Berhad and was a regular invited
speaker to graduates & undergraduates at Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT)
on English Communication Skills & Career Guidance.
Hisham also facilitated and provided consultation services in a 3-day program to
Bousted Penang Shipyard Sdn Bhd during its ‘Transformation & Strategic Business
Planning Retreat’ to all its Heads of Departments which has resulted in the
revelation of various root problems and many key actions being taken into
consideration for implementation.
As an ardent learner of the English Language, his insatiable endeavour to improve
his speaking and writing skills extended to the teaching realm. He became an
English Instructor at a local training firm for Business Communication to working
adults between 2003 and 2005 and to Koperasi Ukhwah in 2011. On a personal
basis, he does Bahasa Malaysia - English (& vice versa) translations from time to
time for many health-product companies. At the same time, he gives advice and
tips to friends and family members in presentation as well as letter writing skills. His
‘Surrounding Sensitivity Series’ are featured on his FB: Champ Hussain weekly.
